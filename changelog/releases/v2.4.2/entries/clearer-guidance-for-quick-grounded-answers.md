`web_answer` is now documented as a quick grounded-answer shortcut for simple factual questions, with clearer guidance to use `web_search` plus `web_contents` when source selection matters and `web_research` for open-ended or multi-step investigations.

This helps agents and users choose the right web tool for the task while keeping `web_answer` available for concise, low-friction answers.
